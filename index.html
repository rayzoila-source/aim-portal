🚀 Deploy to Netlify
Ahmed — complete these steps to get a live shareable URL
STEP 1 — CREATE NETLIFY ACCOUNT
1. Go to netlify.com
2. Click Sign up → use your email
3. Verify your email address
4. You now have a free Netlify account
Create Account →
STEP 2 — DEPLOY THE PORTAL
1. Go to app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag the file AIM_Portal_LIVE.html onto the page
3. Wait 10 seconds
4. Copy the URL Netlify gives you
5. Send that URL to Ray
Go to Netlify Drop →
STEP 3 — UPDATE PORTAL LATER
When you get a new portal file from Claude:
1. Go to app.netlify.com
2. Click your site
3. Click Deploys tab
4. Drag the new HTML file onto the deploy box
5. Same URL — everyone sees the update instantly
